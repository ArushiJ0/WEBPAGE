Projects

Various projects on which our organisation is working on :

  • Food Banks: Collect and distribute food to those in need.
  • Food Pantries: Offer groceries to low-income families.
  • Meal Delivery Programs: Deliver meals to elderly, disabled, or homeless individuals.
  • Food Recovery: Rescue surplus food from restaurants, farms, and markets.
  • Nutrition Education Programs: Teach healthy eating habits, meal planning, and cooking skills.
  • School Feeding Programs: Provide meals to schoolchildren.
  • Food Waste Reduction Apps: Develop apps to reduce food waste.
